# Example Queries for Sprint Summary Chatbot

This document provides example questions you can ask the chatbot to explore your sprint data.

## General Overview

1. "Show me overall sprint summary"
2. "What's the total number of tickets?"
3. "How many sprints do we have?"
4. "Give me a complete overview of the data"
5. "What's the date range of our sprint data?"

## Sprint-Specific Analysis

1. "Summarize Sprint 1" or "Tell me about SPR-001"
2. "What's the status of Sprint 2?"
3. "How many story points are in SPR-003?"
4. "What's the completion rate for Sprint 4?"
5. "Show me all tickets in SPR-001"
6. "Which sprint has the most tickets?"

## Tips for Best Results

- Be specific about sprint IDs (e.g., "SPR-001" instead of just "Sprint 1")
- Use exact status names: "Done", "In Progress", "In Testing", "To Do"
- Use exact priority names: "Critical", "High", "Medium", "Low"
- Combine filters for more precise results
- Request visualizations when you want to see trends
- Ask follow-up questions to drill down into specific areas

## Natural Language Examples

The chatbot understands natural language, so you can ask questions conversationally:

- "Hey, can you show me what Rahul is working on right now?"
- "I need to know which bugs are critical priority"
- "Can you create a chart showing our sprint velocity?"
- "What's the story with our completion rate?"
- "Help me understand team performance this sprint"
- "Give me the full picture of Sprint 2"

Experiment with different phrasings - the AI agent will understand context and provide relevant answers!
